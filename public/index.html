Protected Areas of India

As in May 2012, the protected areas of India cover 156,700 square kilometres (60,500 sq mi), roughly 4.95% of the total surface area.

Popular Statistics

India, a megadiverse country, accounts for 7-8% of all recorded species, including over 91,000 species of animals.

Data Record

Potential secure data hold of the 870 Protected Wildlife Areas in India, opening up networking and reducing wildlife crimes.

Public Outreach

Downloadable formats of data to facilitate knowlege spread among wildlife enthusiasts, encouraging potential research breakthroughs.
